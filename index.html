Our Story

We are a trusted and locally owned company that has been offering unforgettable memories since 2017. We are a small family who dreams of living in our beautiful island Sri Lanka has brought us together to explore Sri Lanka.
We took small steps and create Aminva Leisure with the mission or showing the beauty and diversity of our paradise island and offering small boutique villa lodgings. Everyone is welcome to explore Sri Lanka with us! Weather you are a solo traveler, a group of friends, a family with children or a couple we can tailor the perfect journey for you. A journey with breathtaking landscapes, vibrant wildlife, untouched coastlines, ancient ruins and colonial history, modern cities and timeless rural villages.
You can explore Sri Lankan cuisine rich in colors and spices, or relax your body and your soul with Ayurveda Therapy. We focus on letting experience the true essence of the island like we would love to experiencing our selves. Welcome to our Paradise island and explore with us Sri Lanka!
