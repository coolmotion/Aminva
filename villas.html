Hikkaduwa Aminva Boutique Villa

Aminva Boutique Villa is a contemporary villa in the heart of Hikkaduwa. Few minutes aways from the famous “Turtles Beach” and the Sri Lanka’s first “National Marine Park” Coral Reef. The villa is in a calm and beautiful residential area, Its very well located to facilitate our guests to access to main services such as supermarkets, banks, restaurants, coffee shops. Aminva Boutique Villa was built in 2017 and its latest renovation was in the spring of 2024. With 4 bedrooms and private bathrooms can accommodate up to 10 guests. A wonderful and fully equipped kitchen, spacious living and dining area with an open veranda concept all facing the swimming pool. The rooms can be booked also individually. Breakfast and meals options are available. We have a night security and in house staff in the morning. We aim to create a home far from home.
